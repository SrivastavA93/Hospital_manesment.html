About Us

1. PrimeHealth Super Speciality Hospital, Lucknow (a unit of Starlit Medical Centre Pvt Ltd), is a multidisciplinary 299 bedded tertiary care hospital provides comprehensive medical care across all the medical specialties. Our compassionate care blended with the advanced technology helps our clinicians to deliver superior clinical outcomes in all the disciplines of medical specialities.

2. Experts at PrimeHealth Super Speciality Hospital, Lucknow, have treated more than 6 lakh patients across 39 specialities, including Cardiac sciences, Onco Sciences (Medical and Surgical Oncology), Neurosciences, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Urology, Nephrology, Kidney Transplant, Aesthetic and Reconstructive Surgery and other ancillary services.

3. PrimeHealth Super Speciality Hospital, Lucknow has a complete spectrum of diagnostic and therapeutic technologies including Cardiac Catheterization Laboratory, ICU, Dialysis, advanced radiology & Laboratory services and state of the art modular operating theatres.

4. PrimeHealth Super Speciality Hospital, Lucknow is accredited by National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare providers (NABH) and National Accreditation Board for Laboratories (NABL).
